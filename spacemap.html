Can't Make it to One of These?


Try These Tips!

Get up High:

Get as high up as possible so
buildings don't obstruct your view

Get a Red Flashlight:

Red light has less of an affect
on your eyes than white light

Know when to look:

The best night is crisp and
clear with no humidity

Get Away From the City:

The light pollution from
the city makes the stars harder
to see since it makes the sky
brighter and the makes the stars
appear dimmer in the sky

Dress Warm:

Weather at night can get chilly
especially when staying outside
for extendedperiods of time.
